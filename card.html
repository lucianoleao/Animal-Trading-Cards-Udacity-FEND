Cats

There are cats who have survived falls from over 32 stories (320 meters) onto concrete. They sleep 70% of their lives and a cat was once mayor of Talkeetna, Alaska, for 15 years until his death. His name was Stubbs.

  • Scientifc Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 46 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Present on all continents except Antarctica

They are often called house cats when kept as indoor pets. There are more than seventy cat breeds recognized by various cat registries. Cats are similar in anatomy to the other felids, with a strong flexible body, quick reflexes, sharp retractable claws and teeth adapted to killing small prey.
